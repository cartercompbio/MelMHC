# MelMHC: Autoimmune MHC-I Alleles in Melanoma

Ave - or more classically welcome - gentleperson of erudition. As an astute and avid archaelogist, you are to be commended on your dogged determination, digging into the autoimmune-cancer relationship. In this repository you will find the code needed to reproduce the findings reported by Talwar et al. in [*Autoimmune Alleles at the Major Histocompatibility Locus Modify Melanoma Susceptibility*](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2021.08.12.456166v1.full), a study focused on investigation the relationship between tissue-specific autoimmune associated MHC-I alleles and cancer risk in melanoma.
